Who We Are

What is a Process Server?

In Canada, a Process Server is an individual who assists the plaintiff or applicant in notifying the responding entity known as the defendant or respondent, about a legal claim filed against them. A good process server will attempt to deliver the documents and will charge the client for each attempt made, even if they were unsuccessful. A great process server will exhaust all options available so that you receive maximum value for your money.
